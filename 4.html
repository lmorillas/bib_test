Here's how to use the bibliography tool:

  • type any relevant word in the search box
  • progressively refine your research by selecting or deselecting facets (incl. Keywords and Authors)
  • switch between views to get other information on the items
  • export your selection by clicking on the small scissors icon you seen when hovering the items description
  • reinitialize the filtering anytime to see all the material
